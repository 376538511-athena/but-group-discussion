Attention Is All You Need: Revisiting Transformer Architectures for Efficient NLP
Vaswani A., Shazeer N., Parmar N., Jones U., Gomez A.N., Kaiser L., Polosukhin I.
We propose a new simple network architecture, the Transformer, based solely on attention mechanisms, dispensing with recurrence and convolutions entirely. Experiments on two machine translation tasks show these models to be superior in quality while being more parallelizable and requiring significantly less time to train. Our model achieves 28.4 BLEU on the WMT 2014 English-to-German translation task, improving over the existing best results by over 2 BLEU.

关于 attention head 数量的选择，后续的 BERT 论文做了更详细的 ablation。一般经验是 head 数量取 d_model / d_k，保持每个 head 维度在 64 左右。

周同学@zhouml · 2026-03-19 16:45
从工程实现角度来看，Transformer 的并行化优势非常突出。但是 O(n²) 的空间复杂度在处理长序列时是个瓶颈，后续的 Longformer 和 Reformer 尝试解决这个问题。建议下周可以延伸阅读。
